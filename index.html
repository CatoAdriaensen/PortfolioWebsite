Nice to meet you, I'm Cato

I'm a passionate game designer currently in my final year at Howest - Digitals arts & Entertainment, where improving my skills to create fun, meaningful and memorable games.

I've played games since I was young, and the moment I discovered that I could make them myself, I was all in. I have a love for narrative-driven games, they're in my opinion the best way to tell a story, because you don't just watch the events, you live them.

Of course, I don't shy away from other genres, I've already made and brainstormed lots of games within a wide range of genres, from platformers to puzzle to detective games and more. I can't wait to see what different types of worlds and mechanics I can bring to life!
For me, one of the best feelings is watching people enjoy a game I helped create.

In addition to design, I'm also skilled in project management, being able to keep the project's progress on schedule and maintain clear communication.
